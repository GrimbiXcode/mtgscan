Privacy & Data Protection

What data is processed?

  • Camera images: Only processed locally on your device, not stored or transmitted
  • Card collection: Stored locally in your browser's localStorage
  • API requests: Only collector numbers (e.g., "FDN U 0125") sent to Scryfall API

Your Rights

EU users (GDPR): Rights to access, rectification, erasure, data portability, and objection (Art. 15-21 GDPR)

Other jurisdictions: Local data protection laws apply additionally

No Cookies or Tracking

This app uses no cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Only localStorage for your collection.
